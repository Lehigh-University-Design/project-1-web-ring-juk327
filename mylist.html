A short story about me and my hobby

Growing up, I loved to watch Disney movies and shows on Disney Channel. As I got older, this developed into watching movies and shows in theaters, Netflix, and other streaming platforms.

The peak of my movie and tv-show watching happened during covid, when we were all under quarantine. It was my favorite way to make use of my time and a great way to connect and spend time with my friends through the screen.

Now I watch movies and tv-shows whenever I have the time to.
